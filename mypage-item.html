Why?

The purpose of this website is to demonstrate my ongoing journey of learning and practical application. With over two decades of experience in the industry, I've embarked on the fascinating adventure of creating my own webpage, tackling every intricate detail by myself, which is a novel experience for me.

During my initial venture right after college, I delved into a diverse range of technologies including ASP 3.0, PHP, HTML, Javascript, and even Flash. I played the role of a full-stack engineer, taking charge of designing HTML layouts, implementing CSS and Javascript to ensure seamless functionality across all platforms, and crafting ASP code to render web pages correctly. Additionally, I handled server communication and executed SQL procedures. This era was not only a lot of work but also an immense amount of fun.

As time passed, I became less involved in these technical intricacies, resulting in a bit of rust accumulating. However, I've now embarked on the journey of reclaiming my status as a full-stack engineer. This website will serve as a platform to showcase my continuous learning endeavors and put them into practice. Furthermore, it will provide insights into where I'm currently focusing my energy and expertise.
