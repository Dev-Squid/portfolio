Translation AI Service

AI for translation

Why?

The world of AI is all the rage these days. I've been eager to delve deeper into the realm of artificial intelligence and explore its practical applications.

As the owner of a small indie game studio, I often face certain limitations. One such challenge is the ability to localize our games for different markets. We aspired to take our game, Coffee Shop Tycoon, to new horizons, but the resources required for translation were limited. Being a native Spanish speaker, I decided to embark on a journey to create a solution for translating the game into Spanish.

When I presented this solution to my fellow studio owners, they were genuinely impressed. We collectively agreed to utilize this service for the Spanish translation and are even considering expanding its use to other languages in the future.

What's in My Toolbox?

  • Python: It serves as the backbone of this service.
  • AWS: We rely on AWS for securely storing secrets and potential hosting needs.
  • OpenAI: Our go-to resource for powering up our translation capabilities.
